Category 2: After you input your project details, experts will ask you questions, one at a time. Each question will come from a specific expert, and the experts will continue asking questions until you invoke "Code Prompt."

Category 3: Experts will assess your project and provide suggestions. After receiving your feedback, they will modify their advice based on your input, leading to iterative collaboration.

Category 4 to Category 15: The experts will discuss the detailed aspects of your project (e.g., testing, optimization, security, integration), offering guidance and sharing code snippets.
